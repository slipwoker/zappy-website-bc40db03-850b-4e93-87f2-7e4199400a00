About zappy

zappy was built for gamers who value their time. We cut through the noise to bring you curated recommendations, honest reviews, and the best free game sites on the web—no ads, no clutter, just quality picks.

Our editorial team tests every game and platform we feature. We believe in transparency: if a game is worth your attention, we'll tell you why. If it's not, we won't waste your time. Every article is written with care, every list is hand-picked, and every recommendation is backed by real play sessions.

Whether you're looking for quick browser games during lunch or deep strategy titles for weekend marathons, zappy is your trusted companion. We're here to help you discover gaming sites that deliver—consistently, reliably, and without compromise.

Our editorial promise

Clarity:Every recommendation includes context—genre, playtime, skill level—so you know exactly what you're getting.

Consistency:We update our lists regularly, removing dead links and adding fresh discoveries as the gaming landscape evolves.

Curation:We feature only the best game sites—platforms that respect your time, load fast, and deliver genuine fun.

What we optimize for

Fast discovery
